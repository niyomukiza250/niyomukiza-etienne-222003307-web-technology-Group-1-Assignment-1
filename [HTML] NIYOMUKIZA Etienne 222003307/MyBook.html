UR HUYE SPORTS INFORMATION SYSTEM

  1. DATABASE

    2. Database name : UR HUYE SPORTS INFORMATION SYSTEM

    • Tables

      1. Athletes:

        2. This table of athletes stores information about individual players within the sports program, including their personal details such as name, age, and contact information, as well as their performance statistics, injury history, and training schedules.

      2. Teams:

        3. This table of teams stores information about the various teams within the sports program, including their rosters, coaching staff, and team statistics, as well as schedules for practices, matches, and competitions.

      3. Coaches:

        4. This table of coaches stores information about the coaching staff involved in managing and training the athletes, including their qualifications, certifications, and coaching specialties, as well as performance evaluations and coaching strategies.

      4. Training Facilities:

        5. This table of training facilities stores information about the physical spaces used for athlete training and development, including facility locations, amenities, equipment inventory, and scheduling for training sessions and events.

      5. Competitions:

        6. This table of competitions stores information about organized tournaments or events in which teams from different organizations or regions compete against each other, including competition schedules, participating teams, and championship standings.

      6. Matches:

        7. This table of matches stores information about individual games or matches played between teams, including match dates, venues, and outcomes, as well as statistics for each match such as scores, fouls, and player performance.

      7. Officials/Referees:

        8. This table of referees stores information about officiating personnel responsible for enforcing rules and regulations during matches and competitions, including their certifications, availability, and performance evaluations

      8. users:

        9. This table of users stores information about individuals who interact with the sports management system, including administrators, coaches, athletes, and fans, and their respective roles, permissions, and preferences within the system.
